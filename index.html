About

Muthaiga

Little Lemon, a cozy gem nestled in the heart
of Kenya, where vibrant Mediterranean
and classic Italian flavors come alive. Founded by
two brothers, Adrian and Mario, Little Lemon
blends their passion for food with their unique
talents — Mario’s flair for marketing and
Adrian’s skill in business management. Despite
their different strengths, both brothers are
hands-on chefs, bringing authentic recipes
and heartfelt creativity to every dish.
From zesty lemon-infused seafood to rich
pastas and wood-fired pizzas, each plate
is a celebration of freshness and tradition. At
Little Lemon, every meal feels like a
sunny Mediterranean escape.
